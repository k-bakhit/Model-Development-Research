The mission

Report not just what a model costs, but how confident we are that we know it.

Open models get real GPU measurement. Vendors get quoted as vendors. Closed models get an honest estimate with a range, never a fake exact number. Every chart carries that tag so a reader can tell a fact from a best guess.
