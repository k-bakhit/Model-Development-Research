Sources & Methodology

Where every figure comes from, and how to read it.

Quality tags. Measured = directly measured on known hardware. Vendor-reported = published by the developer (e.g. API prices). Estimated = inferred via a documented formula; shown as ranges, never single numbers.

Disclaimers

1. Independent project, not official. 2. Estimates are ranges. 3. Correlation ≠ causation on the Economy tab. 4. The incomplete current year under-counts recent models. 5. Prices are list/API rates. 6. Energy depends on hardware/batch size and carries large variance. 7. Closed-model inference energy is never measured directly.
