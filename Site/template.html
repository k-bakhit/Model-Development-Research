Sources & methodology

Where every figure comes from, and how to read it.

Quality tags. Measured: directly measured on known hardware. Vendor-reported: published by the developer, such as API prices. Estimated: inferred from a documented formula, shown as ranges and never as single numbers.

Disclaimers

1. Independent project, not official. 2. Estimates are ranges. 3. Correlation is not causation on the Economy tab. 4. The incomplete current year under-counts recent models. 5. Prices are list/API rates. 6. Energy depends on hardware and batch size, and carries large variance. 7. Closed-model inference energy is never measured directly.
